Wild Flour Recipe

Meet Anastasia: Baker and founder of Wild Flour, based in Los Angeles.

Recipe of Life

Growing up in a Greek household, food has always been a part of who she is. There is always something in the oven and you are always welcomed in as a part of the family. Sheâ€™s spent countless hours growing up in the kitchen, with her mother, learning family recipes and cooking techniques. Her father taught her to grow and harvest fruits and vegetables, as well as fish. A strong connection to family and food helped shape her love and appreciation for cooking.

Ingredients to success

At sixteen years old, she taught herself to make sculptural cakes, which eventually led her to enroll in culinary school. She graduated from Le Cordon Bleu in 2011 and began her career in the culinary world.

A Dash of Rus

During her sophomore year of high school she met Ruslana and they quickly realized that the bond that they shared was more than friendship, they were sisters. They have been inseparable ever since. Over the years, Rus has been a pillar of support, always there for anything at anytime. She has been a major driving force in helping Anastasia realize her dream and being able to share her love of baking with everyone. Rus, is Anastasia's second half and the other half of Wild Flour.

Sprinkled with Happiness

Anastasia puts a bit of herself into everything that she does. Her greatest enjoyment comes from sharing a meal shes makes for friends and family. The love, laughter and memories created around a meal, and seeing the people she cares about most, come together is when she is happiest and the most fulfilled.

Flavored with Love

"Food is love - it's like music, you don't have to speak the same language to be able to express an emotion or feeling."
